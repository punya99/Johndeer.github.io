About Us
Our Story

JOHN DEER was the Best company to manufacture All plywood across India. During its long and glorious history, JOHN DEER has sold very large quantities of plywood in every part of India and exported all over the world.

Today JOHN DEER continues its relentless quest to provide customers with the finest quality products.
This is the driving force behind JOHN DEER - the insatiable urge to serve every segment of the wood panel market, giving every application the JOHN DEER edge in quality at the right price.

Our Mission

To consistently evolve in all aspects of manufacturing through innovation and research to create products that add value to our customer's lives and projects while providing superlative customer service that exceeds customer expectations.

Our Vision

To become a global industry leader to influence the world of furnishing, architecture and interior design to set the highest standard of production, provide the best in class customer service, and have minimum impact on our natural resources.

Our Values

We operate with the highest level of integrity, honesty and accountability to earn the trust of our valued customers. We are committed to delivering a flawless experience to our customers, partners, vendors and employees.
